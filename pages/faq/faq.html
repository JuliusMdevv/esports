What is eSports?

Short for “electronic sports”, eSports is just competitive video games. Teams compete against each other in one particular video game. It usually takes place through online tournaments, official eSports teams often compete in a face to face environment. Live commentating and broadcasts enable fans to view and enjoy games as they happen.

What game titles does Poway High School eSports team compete in?

We currently compete in League of Legends, Rocket League, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, and Marvel Rivals

Is eSports really associated with CIF?
